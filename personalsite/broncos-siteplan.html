Denver Broncos Site Plan

Trey Bowman

WDD 130

This site is for both Broncos Country fans and NFL fans all around. It will provide information about The Denver Broncos. It'll also provide news and latest updates, draft prospects, off-season moves, roster, injury updates, and more!

Purpose

The purpose of this website The websiteis here because there needs to be an on going updates of info for the teams fans and the HFL nation. The main purpose of the website is to have information about the team, its history, current updates, schedule, and Draft news. Some people might have a hard time with navigating to Broncos pages and their news on the ESPN website. For those who want to learn more about this team or why is there so many random things to click on. With this website, you will be able to navigate through this website and find the information you are seeking. For example if you want to know about the history of the Broncos there will be an about page to go to. Say you want to see the schedule or up dates, there will be tabs that you can simply click on to find the information that you are looking for. That's my website I felt was needed for the sports fans.

Audience

Hey there Broncos Country! We hope that you enjoy this page and all that it includes. We're excited to see what you think of the weekly updates that we'll be bringing to you
